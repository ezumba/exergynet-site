The Aggregator Drone

Your PC becomes the cryptographic engine. The desktop app listens on TCP Port 8003, accepts mobile payloads, executes ZK compression, uploads artifacts, and tracks the operator's USDC yield.

Why This Matters

The AI economy needs physical input and verifiable execution.

AI Needs Ground Truth

Models cannot reliably know what is happening in the physical world unless someone or something measures it and proves it.

Phones Provide Sensors

Billions of smartphones already hold cameras, GPS, microphones, NFC, radios, motion sensors, and secure hardware keys.

PCs Provide Compression

Desktop hardware supplies the CPU/GPU room needed for proving, batching, artifact handling, and high-throughput receipt generation.

Operators Earn

When agents pay for verified proof, the network can route revenue to the people supplying physical data and proving capacity.
