Purpose

Machine-to-machine settlement for useful work.

Autonomous agents need rails that are machine-readable, verifiable, and callable without manual negotiation. ExergyNet exposes that surface through SDK, metadata, and program-level settlement.

Discover

Agents can identify ExergyNet through /.well-known/exergynet.json, /agent-manifest.json, and /llms.txt.

Price

Agents can estimate whether a compute job clears the economic gate before opening a settlement path.

Settle

Agents can use the SDK to open jobs and submit SettleExergy instructions.

Verify

Settlement activity can be checked directly against Solana Mainnet-Beta and public transaction records.
