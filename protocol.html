Consensus

Proof-of-Exergy

PoX replaces Proof-of-Work (hash waste) and Proof-of-Stake (capital plutocracy) with a thermodynamically-anchored truth primitive. Physical entropy generation is the proof of work.

Exergy Definition

In thermodynamics, exergy is the maximum useful work possible during a process that brings a system into equilibrium with a heat reservoir. ExergyNet binds CPU thermal flux (measured in millidegrees Celsius) to the cryptographic verification of each compute payload.
