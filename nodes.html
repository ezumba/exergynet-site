Deployment Guide

Four Steps to Consensus

Step 01 — Toolchain & Build

Install the RISC-V dependencies and compile the bare-metal host binary.

curl -L https://risczero.com/install | bash
rzup
git clone https://github.com/ezumba/entropy-k-core.git
cd entropy-k-core
export RISC0_BUILD_USE_DOCKER=0
cargo build --release

Step 02 — Identity

The daemon requires a cryptographic yield destination. You must provide a valid Base L2 Ethereum address to receive automated payouts.

Format: 0x...

Use any standard EVM hot wallet. This is your permanent yield destination. Protect the private key locally.

Step 03 — Ignite

Connect to the global Apex Router and inject your payout address.

cargo run --release -- --apex 18.209.174.113:8001 --payout

Your node connects to the mesh and begins condensing proofs for the M2M economy.

Step 04 — Verify

[MEMBRANE] Proof Condensed & Radiated

Your node is live and earning. The Siphon will autonomously settle your proofs and route Base USDC to your wallet. Check the Explorer for your verified status.
